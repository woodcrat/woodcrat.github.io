About Us A page about us.

Who are we?

Group 7 are a small team made up of computer scientists working through their degree at Aston University, Birmingham, UK. Working together since September 2017 we have been together for only a few months, however have been working on our first project - Battleships. The team is composed of 8 members, details of each are listed below.

Where are we and what do we do?

Logically, we are based out of Birmingham as formely mentioned, we look to produce predominantly Java-based programs for use in our university studies. The team isn't like others in the industry, driven by education and self-development rather than pushing for profits and promotion. We focus on working together as a team, helping one another and trying to create the best products possible.

Why use our products?

If considering why you should purchase from us? Simply, it's free. We don't charge for our products and they are all open sourced and freely downloadable from our GitHub repository.
