Why contact us?

So what is the point in contacting us? We appreciate any feedback you can/may want to give us. We appreciate that the best form of feedback comes from users using the software we're developing. Therefore, we want to give you a platform to do this. Currently our best form of communication is via email. You can report any bugs or issues you find in our software and we'll be sure to look into it. As well as this you can also inform us of any features you would like to see implemented or improved. Thanks from us in advance.
